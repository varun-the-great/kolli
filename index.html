About Kolli hills
Attractions
Koli hills is a offbeat hill station which is full of many breathtaking attractions and sites that won't make your trip feel boring. There are outstanding and ancient temples like the -- with hidden secrets and all of them are unique. There are many waterfalls that have great views and can cool off your day with constant rains of water coming from many natural rivers. There are also many famous view points that show the amazing mountain range and the ground down below. This will be a 3 to 4 trip because of the limited tourist attractions.
